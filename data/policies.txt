Personal Loan Policy:
Personal loans require valid KYC documents such as Aadhaar, PAN card, and income proof.
The loan tenure ranges from 1 to 5 years.
Interest rates vary based on the applicant's credit score and income.

Home Loan Policy:
Home loans are available for purchase, construction, or renovation of residential property.
Maximum tenure is up to 30 years.
Applicants must provide property documents, income proof, and identity proof.

Education Loan Policy:
Education loans are provided for higher studies in India and abroad.
A co-applicant (parent or guardian) is mandatory.
Repayment starts after course completion plus a moratorium period.

Vehicle Loan Policy:
Vehicle loans are available for both two-wheelers and four-wheelers.
Loan tenure can range from 1 to 7 years.
Vehicle registration and insurance documents are required.

KYC Policy:
KYC is mandatory for all banking services.
Documents include Aadhaar card, PAN card, passport, or voter ID.

Transaction Policy:
Transactions above a certain limit may require additional verification.
Suspicious transactions may be temporarily blocked for security reasons.
